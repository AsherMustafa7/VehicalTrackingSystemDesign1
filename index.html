About the Solution

FLEETMitra

DigiPass/FleetMitra is a state-of-the-art Vehicle Monitoring System (VMS) developed by the Directorate of Geology and Mining, Government of Uttar Pradesh. It delivers secure, paperless, and transparent mineral transportation across the state. By leveraging AIS-140 compliant VTD devices, geo-fencing, real-time monitoring via track its location, movement and status. DigiPass ensures that every mineral movement is legal, trackable, and seamless.

KeyOutcomes

Real-time monitoring of mineral carrying vehicles from Source to Destination

Ensures the issuance of transit passes only from the lease area

Automatic trip closing by the system after the vehicle reaches the destination

Issuance of next transit pass (eMM-11/FORM-C/ISTP) only after expiring the previous transit pass

Accurate vehicle breakdown reporting.

Major route deviations alerts during mineral transportation through mCheck app.

Identification of hidden routes being used by drivers to avoid check gates.

Real-time alerts to concern stakeholders when VTS fails/tampered/emergency button pushed.

Control of illegal transportation thereby curbing illegal mining.

More accurate estimation of Transit Pass validity timeline after analysis of VTS data.

Actual active mines Information in the state from data analysis of TP issuance & vehicle movements.

District wise MineTag installation report with the geo-location evidence.

Objective

A Vehicle Tracking System (VTS) is a GPS-based system that is installed in a vehicle to monitor and track its location in real time. We can track vehicles real-time on 24/7 basis through computers, smart phones, etc.

End-To-End tracking of mineral carrying vehicles in the state

Ensuring that vehicles are operating within designated mining zones and adhering to regulations

Promote legal mineral transportation
